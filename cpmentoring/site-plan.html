Overview

Purpose

The purpose of the website is to serve as a comprehensive online platform for our company, offering personalized English classes and educational mentoring services. Through the website, our primary purpose is to provide detailed and relevant information about our personalized language classes, mentoring programs and teaching methodology. We aim to educate visitors about the benefits of our services and how they can achieve their language learning and personal growth goals through our tailored approach, emphasizing the value and advantages of personalized learning plans and educational mentoring. By highlighting how our tailored approach addresses the unique needs, strengths, and weaknesses of each student or client, we foster a supportive and growth-oriented environment that helps individuals thrive in their language learning journey.

Audience

Our desired audience comprises individuals who are seeking personalized English classes and educational mentoring services to enhance their language skills and achieve personal growth. This includes non-native English speakers looking to improve their language proficiency, students who require academic support, professionals seeking to advance their careers, and individuals who are interested in expanding their knowledge and self-improvement. Our audience values quality education and personalized support, appreciating the benefits of tailored learning plans and individualized attention. They are motivated to succeed in their language learning journey and are eager to find a supportive and growth-oriented environment that addresses their unique needs, strengths, and weaknesses.

Programs

PAGE 2 - PROGRAMS
Program 1 - TAILORED LEARNING
Every learner is unique, with distinct strengths, weaknesses, and learning preferences. That's why our tailored approach classes are designed to provide a customized learning experience that helps you achieve your language learning goals effectively.
Our experienced teachers will work closely with you to assess your current proficiency level and understand your specific objectives. Based on this assessment, they will create a personalized learning plan that addresses your individual needs, ensuring that you receive targeted instruction and practice in areas where you need the most improvement.
We employ a variety of engaging teaching methods, interactive materials, and real-life scenarios to make your learning experience immersive, practical, and enjoyable. Through our tailored approach, you will not only develop your language skills but also gain the confidence to communicate effectively in English.
Experience the advantages of personalized learning plans and embark on a language learning journey that is perfectly suited to your unique requirements. Join us today and unlock your full potential in English.
Program 2 - 75 HARD ENGLISH
Inspired by Frisella's renowned 75 Hard Challenge, the 75 Hard English is an intensive English learning program designed to help you establish a consistent and focused study routine that accelerates your language proficiency. By committing to these rules for 75 consecutive days, you will experience rapid growth in your English skills, boost your confidence, and achieve outstanding results.
The program entails a rigorous daily regimen, including immersive language practice, targeted vocabulary and grammar exercises, listening comprehension, and speaking activities. Additionally, you will engage in regular reading sessions, writing practice, and self-reflection to reinforce your learning.
By embracing the 75 Hard English Program, you will immerse yourself in a transformative language learning journey. This intensive approach challenges you to push your limits, overcome obstacles, and develop discipline. Throughout the program, you will witness substantial progress, enhanced fluency, and an expanded vocabulary.
If you are ready to take your English language learning to the next level, join us and embark on this transformative 75-day challenge. Amplify your dedication, experience accelerated results, and unlock your true potential in English. The journey starts now.
Program 3 - EDUCATIONAL MENTORING
At CPMentoring, we offer comprehensive educational mentoring services to support your language learning and personal growth. We understand that achieving fluency in English involves more than just learning vocabulary and grammar; it requires guidance, motivation, and a supportive environment.
Our dedicated mentors are passionate about helping you succeed. They will work closely with you to understand your goals, strengths, and weaknesses, providing personalized guidance and support throughout your language learning journey. Our mentors are not only language experts but also skilled in personal development strategies, empowering you to overcome challenges, set realistic goals, and stay motivated.
Through our educational mentoring program, you will receive one-on-one mentoring sessions tailored to your specific needs. Our mentors will help you identify and address areas for improvement, provide valuable feedback, and equip you with effective learning strategies to enhance your language skills.
With our educational mentoring services, you will benefit from a holistic approach to language learning that nurtures your personal growth alongside your linguistic development. Join us today to experience the transformative power of educational mentoring and take your language proficiency to new heights.

About Me

PAGE 3 - ABOUT ME
Hello! I am Carolina Pereira and I am an Educational Mentor. I believe that education is essential for individual and social development and I am dedicated to helping people achieve their goals through learning new languages.
I believe in the importance of education to transform people's lives and make them more self-reliant. I believe that each individual has the potential to become a better version of themselves, and I strive to help my students and mentees achieve that potential. As a member of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, I also believe in the power of faith and prayer to guide us and help us overcome life's challenges.
I have over 25 years of experience in language teaching, having worked in language schools and as a private tutor and mentor. During this time, I have developed skills in lesson planning, effective communication with students, and adaptation to each individual's needs.
I completed my undergraduate degree in Philosophy (yes, Philosophy!) at State University of Campinas in 2001. Since then, I have participated in several courses and workshops to stay up-to-date on language teaching trends and techniques. I am currently pursuing studies in software development. My decision to study software development was driven by my desire to make education more accessible and to promote better outcomes through the use of technology.
I have skills in language teaching, lesson planning, and effective communication with students from different levels and backgrounds. I am also fluent in Portuguese, English, and I developing Spanish and French.
I have participated in various projects to help individuals develop themselves through the Self-Reliance program of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, which includes an English language teaching program and a career education program. In addition, I have assisted numerous students in preparing for international language proficiency exams, such as Cambridge, TOEFL and IELTS. I am passionate about using my skills and experience to empower individuals to achieve their goals and fulfill their potential.
Besides language teaching, I am a person interested in music, theater, and travel. I enjoy exploring new places and cultures, and sharing these experiences with my friends, students and mentees.
